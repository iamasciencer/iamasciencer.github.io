About me

Who am I?

Hi, it's me, Lukas!

Originally from a small town in Carinthia in southern Austria, I decided to leave for Vienna and pursue studies in the field of astronomy.

Besides my work on my research interests, I have multiple other interests that try to follow.

These interests vary from time to time and in intensity, but in general, you can say that I am interested in the following things (among others)

  • History: especially military history from the middle ages forward
  • Reading: I am trying to catch up on some literature classics like "Dracula" or the works of H. P. Lovecraft
  • Movies: If done well, the adaptations of books to movies can be a really good way to relax (especially Lord of the Rings or Harry Potter)
  • Eating and drinking: There is a good chance that I will not decline good food and drinks (preferably beer, although I've come to like a few scottish and irish whiskys as well)
  • Animals: I am a cat person! Although, every animal that is fluffy and friendly is welcome to get a few pat-pats ;)
